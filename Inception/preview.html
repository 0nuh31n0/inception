Inception is a 2010 science fiction
action film written and directed by
Christopher Nolan, who produced the
film with his wife, Emma Thomas.

The film stars Leonardo DiCaprio as a
professional thief who steals
information by infiltrating the
subconscious, and is offered a chance
to have his criminal history erased as
payment for the implantation of
another person's idea into a target's
subconscious.
